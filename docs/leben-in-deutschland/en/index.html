Important information

Not a government app

This is a private educational project, not an official app of the Federal Office for Migration and Refugees (BAMF). The app is not affiliated with any government authorities and does not represent any official institutions.

Source of the questions

The test questions are based on the official BAMF question catalog. The original content is published in German in the BAMF online test center.

About the translations

The translations are AI-generated to support users with limited knowledge of German and may contain inaccuracies. If you believe a translation should be improved, you can notify the developer directly from the app. Only the original German text of the questions is authoritative.
